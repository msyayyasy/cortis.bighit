ABOUT

CORTIS (MARTIN, JAMES, JUHOON, SEONGHYEON, and KEONHO) is the first boy band in 6 years to debut under BIGHIT MUSIC, the powerhouse label behind iconic global groups like BTS and TOMORROW X TOGETHER. The name CORTIS is an unconventional mix of six letters drawn from the phrase “COLOR OUTSIDE THE LINES,” reflecting their determination to think beyond the boundaries defined by the world.

Officially Debuted on August 18, 2025.

MARTIN

Martin (마틴), also known as Martin Edwards and Park Woo-joo, was born on March 20, 2008. He is the leader of CORTIS and holds Korean-Canadian nationality. He stands approximately 190.5 cm tall, has blood type O, and his MBTI is INFP/ENFP. His representative color is red and his emoji is a dog. He was born in Seoul, South Korea, and has lived in Canada.

JAMES

James (제임스), whose full name is Chao Yufan or James Chao, was born on October 14, 2005. He is the oldest member of CORTIS and has Thai-Taiwanese nationality. His height is around 176–178 cm, his blood type is A, and his MBTI is INTP. His representative color is green and his emoji is an eagle. He is multilingual and has experience as a trainee prior to debut.

JUHOON

Juhoon (주훈), born Kim Ju-hoon on January 3, 2008, is a Korean member of CORTIS. He is approximately 175–176 cm tall, has blood type O, and his MBTI is ISFP. His representative color is blue and his emoji is a turtle. He is known for being a former child model and can speak both Korean and English.

SEONGHYEON

Seonghyeon (성현), whose full name is Eom Seong-hyeon (Sean Eom), was born on January 13, 2009. He is Korean, stands around 175 cm tall, and has blood type A. His MBTI is INTP, his representative color is black, and his emoji is a fox. He is known for his long trainee period and involvement in music production.

KEONHO

Keonho (건호), also known as An Keon-ho, was born on February 14, 2009. He is the youngest member (maknae) of CORTIS and is Korean. He is approximately 178 cm tall, has blood type O, and his MBTI is INTP. His representative color is yellow and his emoji is a puppy. Before becoming a trainee, he was known as a swimming athlete.
